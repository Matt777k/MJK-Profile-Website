About Me "Coding Dietitian"

I began my journey by pursuing a career in the health field. After a year in college I developed an interest in nutrition science. Fast forward a few years I had graduated college, completed my internship, and had been working as a Registered Dietitian. After some time I began to feel like I had more to contribute to the field and felt limited by my knowledge and skill set. I have always had a strong passion for technology and as time goes on it has become more and more apparent that technology is an integral part of the future of our healthcare system. This realization is what pushed me to furthur my skill set and learn software development. I knew with this knowledge I could combine my passions of health and technology in order to help craft the future of healthcare technology.
